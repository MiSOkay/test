Legend
- Command ⌘
- Shift ⇧
- Option ⌥
- Control ⌃

Various
- ⌥ F12: toggle the terminal tool window visibility

Bookmarks
- F3: toggle bookmark
- ⌥ F13: toggle bookmark with mnemonic
- ⌃0...⌃9: go to numbered bookmark
- ⌘ F3: show bookmarks

Breakpoints
- ⌘ F8: toggle breakpoint
- ⌘ ⇧ F8: view breakpoints
